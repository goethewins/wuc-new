Step 1 ---> How to get a Bitcoin wallet

Setting up your first Bitcoin wallet is simple with several quality mobile apps available.

A great path would be (1) a mobile wallet like Airbitz (iPhone, Android), Breadwallet (iPhone) or Mycelium (Android), (2) acquire bitcoins from a friend, as payment for a good or service or buy bitcoins from Circle and then (3) if you decide to store large amounts of bitcoins and want to know they are absolutely safe then use Armory.

Best Mobile Bitcoin Wallets

iPhone and Android apps are available with popular choices including:

  • Airbitz (Android, iPhone)
  • Breadwallet (iPhone)
  • Mycelium (Android)

Best Bitcoin Software Wallets

Bitcoin software wallets are downloaded to your computer, give you more control and do not depend on third-party services. Most software bitcoin wallets require a day or so to download the blockchain before they are ready for use.

  • Bitcoin Core - The best option for network security.
  • Armory - (Highly Recommended) Armory is the most mature, secure and full featured Bitcoin wallet but it can be technologically intimidating for users. Whether you are an individual storing $1,000 or institution storing $1,000,000,000 this is the most secure option available. Users are in complete control all Bitcoin private keys and can setup a secure offline-signing process in Armory.

Best Bitcoin Web Wallets

Bitcoin web wallets are the easiest and most convenient to use but are potentially less secure than the above options because the private keys to your bitcoins are usually held by a third-party.

  • Circle

Best Bitcoin Hardware Wallets

Bitcoin hardware wallets are the most secure because they do not expose your private keys to the network.

  • Trezor

What Is Bitcoin Mining?

Mining bitcoins requires an investment in specialized bitcoin mining hardware designed to process double round sha256 hash verifications at high speed. Getting started with Bitcoin mining can be a daunting endeavor and it has become highly specialized so you may want to leave it to professionals. Nevertheless, there are many excellent bitcoin mining systems available.

It is important to point out that bitcoin mining is highly competitive and risky for would-be participants. Please see our bitcoin mining guide for more detail.
